Fried Butter

Description

A healthy salad after you've just given yourself a heart attack by eating some fried butter.

Ingredients

  • Lettuce
  • Tomato
  • Onion
  • Cucumber
  • Olive Oil
  • Balsamic Vinegar

Steps

  1. Chop ingredients and place them into a bowl
  2. Pour over balsamic vinegar and olive oil
  3. Mix the salad thoroughly
  4. It's a salad.
